Get To Know Me

My name is Brian Adams, and I'm from Cincinnati, Ohio. I am currently in my third of five years as a Computer Science student at the University of Cincinnati. Along with being a full-time student, I am a full stack developer, specializing in modern web technologies. My primary focus is on Javascript, Python, and C# development. I have been working professionally since 2012, both as a consultant and a regular employee. I have written web applications using Python/Flask, Node.js, .NET Web Forms, .NET MVC, and .NET Web API. I work frequently with AngularJS for client-side code as well as jQuery and plain JavaScript.

While not writing code or studying, I enjoy sports and music. I am an avid Cincinnati Bearcat and Cincinnati Reds fan. I also love basketball, mostly the NBA. I am a founding father of the Beta Omicron chapter of Theta Chi Fraternity, and I am also a member of the ACM chapter of UC.
